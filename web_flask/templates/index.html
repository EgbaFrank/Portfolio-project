About GroceryHub

GroceryHub was inspired by the need to make grocery shopping simpler and more affordable. During my own experience of visiting multiple stores to find the best prices, I realized how inefficient it was. This sparked the idea for a platform that consolidates grocery lists, prices, and stores in one place.
